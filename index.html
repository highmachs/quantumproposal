Clock Domain: Phase Alignment

Distributed systems drift. Standard NTP/PTP allows the clock to drift significantly before a "step" correction. The Hybrid FPGA uses a Digital Phase Locked Loop (DPLL) to continuously steer the clock, maintaining lock.

Strategic Value for HFT

Deterministic Order Books

Ensures incoming market data packets are processed in the exact order of arrival on the wire, preventing "ghost" liquidity signals caused by OS-scheduler reordering.

Latency Arbitrage

The 2-3µs saved by bypassing the OS stack and using Cut-Through switching provides the edge needed to capture price discrepancies between exchanges before competitors.

Audit & Compliance

Hardware timestamps provide irrefutable proof of "Time of Trade" for MiFID II compliance, with nanosecond resolution that software clocks cannot legally guarantee.
